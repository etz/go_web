Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information when you register on our site, log in with Steam, or fill out a form. Information collected includes your name, Steam ID, email address, and profile information provided by Steam.

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect from you in the following ways:

  • To personalize your experience and deliver content most relevant to you
  • To improve our website based on the feedback and information we receive from you
  • To process transactions and provide requested services
  • To send periodic emails regarding your account or other products and services

3. Cookies

We use cookies to understand and save your preferences for future visits and compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer better site experiences in the future.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties unless we provide users with advance notice. This does not include website hosting partners and other parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or serving our users, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

5. Steam Authentication

Our site uses Steam authentication to verify user identities. When you log in with Steam, we receive basic profile information from your Steam account. We store this information to provide you with our services. We do not have access to your Steam credentials.

6. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your personal information is contained behind secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights to such systems.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.
